## CHALLENGES
Intro: To scale the platform effectively, I focused on resolving key challenges across experience, systems and collaboration.
Card 1 — Disconnected Experience: No unified interaction model. UX inconsistencies across mobile and web.
Card 2 — No Scalable Design System: No shared component library. Repeated efforts and inconsistent visuals.
Card 3 — Inefficient Collaboration: No standard handoff process. Limited documentation led to misalignment.

## DESIGNED FOR CLARITY
Heading: Designed for Clarity
Body: Every screen organized around what the user needs to do next, not just what the system needs to show.
Captions:
1. Organized around the actions a broker needs to take, not just the data the system holds.
2. Context stays visible while edits happen — no navigation away, no lost orientation.
3. Financials structured from summary to detail — so the right number is always one glance away.
4. A guided flow that keeps brokers oriented at every step, from start to submission.
5. Field-level controls that let brokers configure templates without touching anything they shouldn't.
6. Policy details, formatted and ready — previewed before it reaches the member.
7. Multiple insurers, one view — with a clear recommendation so the decision doesn't stall.
8. Health inputs assessed in real time — so brokers know what's viable before the quote goes out.

## DESIGNED FOR EVERY ROLE
Heading: Designed for Every Role
Body: Two distinct users, two distinct experiences — designed around what each role actually needs to see and do.
Captions:
1. Key numbers and member actions surfaced upfront — so HR can manage on the go without digging.
2. Claims, nominees and account details — each task in its own space, nothing competing for attention.
3. The full benefits experience in a white-labeled app — policies, requests and health perks, all in one place.
4. The platform extended beyond insurance — branded and configured for how each partner wants to sell.

## ENTERPRISE DESIGN SYSTEM
Heading: Enterprise Design System
Body: With the product being white-labeled across multiple partners, I worked within a neutral visual language — balancing muted tones with modular components, adaptable grids and flexible layouts to ensure easy adaptability across use cases. This system accelerated design-developer collaboration and future-proofed the platform for scalable growth.
